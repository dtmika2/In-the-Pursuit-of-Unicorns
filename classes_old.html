In the Pursuit of Unicorns

Playable Classes

Jester

Rogue · Bard · Melee Crit · Stealth

Wit +2

You are the chaos at the heart of every encounter. You do not fight — you make fighting unnecessary, impossible, or hilarious. And on the rare occasion you must bleed someone, you make it look like an accident.

Abilities
Mocking Cackle Roll d20 + Wit · Target DC 12

Your mocking laugh gets under their skin. Target makes a save or cannot attack next turn — too busy fuming, or, worse, giggling. If a target is affected by mocking laugh 3 times consecutively, it start harming itself. The laugh is insuffarable, and it needs to stop.

Trick Blade Roll d20 + Wit

A deceptive melee strike, rolled with advantage. Throw a d12. Different rolls have different outcomes.

Shadow Step DC 15 to detect · Ends on full sprint

Melt into shadow or a crowd. You become nearly invisible — DC 15 to detect — and can move at half speed while hidden. Breaking into a full sprint ends the effect immediately.

Pocket of Wonders Once per scene · Roll d6

Reach into your endless pockets. Roll a d6 — you never know, what your own pockets can hide.

Jester's Medley Once per scene · Choose one effect

A performance, a jape, a jest. Inspire one ally — they gain advantage on their next roll. Or: Distract all enemies in sight, they all gain -1 to their next attack throw. Outside of a combat, Jester's Medley causes drawn attention to you. Every mesmerized creature pays almost no attention to what is happening around them.

Dreamcaster

Utility · Crowd Control · Charisma

Charisma +2

You weave the dream-stuff of this world into veils, voices, and visions. Where others reach for steel or fire, you reach into minds. You are most at home in the space between sleep and waking.

Abilities
Minor Illusion Unlimited uses

Create a small visual or auditory illusion — a sound, a shadow, a drifting light, a fleeting face in the fog. No larger than a person. No direct combat effect. Limited only by your imagination. You may use this freely, as many times as you wish.

Telepathy Sending · Reading: d20 + Charisma

Reach into a mind within 30 feet. Send a wordless message — emotion, image, intent — silently and instantly. Or read their surface thoughts: what they are focused on right now. The target is unaware in either case.

Mesmerize Once per scene · DC 13 Charisma save or Charmed 1 round

Lock eyes with one target. If they fail their save, they are Charmed for 1 round — they cannot act against you or your allies. They remember being held. They do not know why.

Magic Mouth Automatic · Lasts until triggered or dismissed

Enchant a surface, object, humanoid or wall with a stored message. Set a trigger — when someone crosses a threshold, speaks a name, or touches the object. When triggered, a magical mouth delivers your words exactly as you spoke them.

Puppet Once per scene · DC 14 Charisma save or controlled 1 action

For one terrifying moment, you take the wheel. Force one creature within 30 feet to take a single action of your choosing. They do it. They cannot stop it. They know it was you.

Ranger

Scout · Ranged Combat · Herbalist

Instinct +2

You read the land. You read the people. Where others are lost to the bliss, you find the thread. You keep moving. You are the only one in this world who is still truly looking.

Abilities
Precise Shot Roll d20 + Instinct to hit · 1d10 damage · Ignores cover

Take careful aim before firing. Your next ranged attack deals 1d10 damage and ignores all cover bonuses. The arrow finds the gap.

Volley Once per scene · Roll d20 + Instinct per target · 1d6 each

Loose a flurry of arrows at up to 3 different targets simultaneously. Roll separately for each target. Each hit deals 1d6 damage. For one moment, the air is full of fletching.

Roped Shot Roll d20 + Instinct to hit · DC 13 Strength to break free

Fire an arrow with a string attached. Immidiately after succesful shot, you can pull the target 3 feets closer. On every next turn, you can try to disballance the target, throwing it onto a ground - until the sting is cut.

Herbalist Passive · Always active

Identify any herb, drink, fruit, insect before it is consumed — its effects, potency, and DC. When you consume a herb, halve chance to a negative effect. You know what grows in this dark, and what it does to people. You can also make ointment and dip your arrows in it - targets of these arrows suffer the cosecvences of that source.

Ranger's Eye Tracking: Always succeeds · Danger sense: d20 + Instinct vs DC 11

You always find the trail — footprints, broken stems, magical residue, lingering scent. Hidden paths reveal themselves. Additionally, you sense hidden dangers, traps, and unnatural presences within 30 feet. You feel the wrongness before you can name it. +3 to all perception throws for 10 minutes.

Shapeshifter

Tank · Melee · Infiltration

Shift +2

You are not one thing. You never were. In a world that has forgotten itself entirely, you remember every shape you have ever worn — and a few that haven't been invented yet.

Abilities
Beast Form Roll d20 + Shift vs DC 11 · Lasts 10 min

Transform fully into one of four preset forms. You keep your mind, but gain the animal's body, senses, and instincts. Lasts up to 10 minutes.

Mimic Roll d20 + Shift · DC 14 Insight to see through

Copy the exact appearance, voice, and mannerisms of any humanoid you have closely observed. Others must make a DC 14 Insight check to notice something is off. A perfect copy — down to the nervous habit.

Partial Shift + Predator Sense Automatic · Lasts 1 hour · No surprise

Grow one animal feature — claws (1d6 melee), gills, a prehensile tail, or a climbing grip — and simultaneously borrow a set of animal senses: echolocation, heat-sight, a wolf's nose, or an owl's night-vision. You cannot be surprised while active.

Regenerate Once per rest · Recover 1d8 HP

Shift your flesh to push out damage and knit wounds shut. Your body ripples briefly, disturbingly — then settles back to normal.

Grotesque Form Once per scene · DC 13 save or Frightened 1 round

Shift into something wrong. Limbs too many, eyes too wide, angles that shouldn't exist. All enemies who can see you must save or be Frightened for 1 round — they cannot move toward you and have -2 to all rolls. Your allies are unaffected.

Sorcerer

Magic Damage · Volatile Power

Power +2

Magic does not flow through you. It erupts. You are a pressure vessel of arcane force, and this corrupted world has made that pressure volatile. Everything you do carries a cost — and sometimes, so does everyone standing near you.

Abilities
Hex Target DC 13 save or hexed for 1 round

Curse an enemy with magical affliction. They have disadvantage — roll twice, take the worse result — on all their rolls for 1 full round.

Arcing Bolt d20 + Power to hit first · 1d10 → 1d6 → 1d4 per jump

Launch a bolt that leaps between enemies. It hunts. Roll to hit the first target (1d10 on hit). The bolt then automatically jumps to the nearest enemy within 10 feet (1d6), then once more (1d4). It cannot return to a previously struck target.

Void Touch Melee · d20 + Power vs DC 12 · 2d4 force + Pinned

Reach out by hand. The air around a target compresses with vicious invisible force. On hit: 2d4 force damage. Target starts wandering aimlessly for 3 turns.

Gravity well Once per scene · Roll d20 first · DC 13 or 2d6 to all within 15 ft

Release a burst of volatile magic — uncontrolled, indiscriminate. All creatures within 15 feet, including allies, must save (DC 13) or start floating. Floating creatures cannot attack or cast spells. They can swim in air 6 feet per turn. After 3 turns, creatures fall. Before you use it, roll d20: on a 1–5, you are caught in your own blast as well.

Arcane Fortress Once per scene · Lasts for concentration

Summon a solid 2×2×2 cube of shimmering force anywhere you can see. It is real and physical. Creatures may stand inside it (full cover from ranged attacks), stand on top, or be blocked entirely by its walls. Lasts until dismissed or you fall unconscious.
