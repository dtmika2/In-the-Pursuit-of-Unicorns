In the Pursuit of Unicorns · Homebrew
How Sub-Skills Work

When a roll clearly falls within a sub-skill's scope, add +1 to the total. This stacks with your ability modifier.

d20 + modifier (+2) + sub-skill (+1) = d20 + 3

The DM decides whether a sub-skill applies. When in doubt: if you would describe what you're doing using the sub-skill's name, it applies.

While creating your character, you can choose 2 sub-skills from your class and one from your race. These gain +1 modifier above your ability modifier. The rest match modifier of the ability.
